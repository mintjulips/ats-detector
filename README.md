# ats-detector

The purpose of ATS Detector is to help job candidates group applications by ATS to speed up their application process without losing quality or overwhelming hiring teams like with automatic job application subscription models.

# problem statement
Job candidates must send out hundreds and thousands of applications to receive interviews. Meanwhile, hiring teams are inundated by applications, most of which will be archived. New business models offer job candidates subscription services to apply to multiple jobs at once. Nation state actors are exploiting ATSes as attack vectors. All of this creates a disconnect and a widening gap between job candidates and hiring teams. 

# who this is for
Job candidates looking to improve their speed sending out applications without sacrificing quality or paying for subscription models.

# how it works
Go to the job description, copy the URL, paste it into the ATS Detector form, and ATS Detector will tell you what ATS the company is using. This will help you to group your applications by ATS type and this will save you time without sacrificing quality or risk getting you blacklisted.
